About King + Bunk pods

Our most popular style and the one with the most pods. A king-size bed and a bunk sit in one warm, open-plan space, which makes these pods brilliant for a couple bringing their dog, or a family of four who like to be together.

Like every Tonnage Bridge pod, you get your own wood-fired hot tub on the water and a canoe launch on the doorstep. Five pods share this style: Water Vole, Moor Hen, Shrew, Kingfisher and Otter.

What is inside

  • Wood-fired hot tub
  • Full kitchen
  • Shower room & WC
  • Smart TV & Wi-Fi
  • Fire pit with grill
  • Canoe launch on the doorstep

Pods in this style: Water Vole, Moor Hen, Shrew, Kingfisher and Otter. One well-behaved dog welcome at £20 per stay.
