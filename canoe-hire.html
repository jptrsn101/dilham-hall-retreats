On the water

Our own quiet canal, all to yourself.

Set off from our private launch on the North Walsham and Dilham Canal and glide through reed beds, past kingfishers and dragonflies. It is calm, shallow and traffic free, which makes it perfect for first timers and families as well as seasoned paddlers.

Every hire comes with buoyancy aids, paddles sized to you, and a friendly safety briefing before you go. Dry bags are available if you would like to keep things dry.

  • Buoyancy aids included

Good to know

Canoe hire questions.

Do I need any experience?

Not at all. The canal is calm, shallow and traffic free, and you get a safety briefing before you set off. It suits complete beginners and families.

Can children come paddling?

Yes. Children are welcome with adult supervision and are fitted with buoyancy aids. Get in touch if you would like advice on the right craft for your group.

What is included in the price?

Buoyancy aids, paddles sized to you, and a safety briefing. Dry bags are available to hire if you would like to keep belongings dry.

Can I bring my own canoe or paddleboard?

Yes. Self-launch is £10 per vessel per day from our launch. Pick up a lanyard for each boat when you arrive.

What if I want to book on the day?

Call us on 07810 616920 to check availability for same day hire.
