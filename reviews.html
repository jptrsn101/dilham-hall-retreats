Me and my mum stayed at Dilham Hall Retreats with my 2 young boys. It was so idyllic! Perfect, quiet setting on the canal where we had plenty of open space. My boys loved the space to play around and explore and they especially loved the hot tub and being able to ‘light’ the wood so it made it a hands on experience. It’s a lovely location, with the broads 15 mins away and the coast also 15 mins.

The pod was so clean and had everything we needed to make it a comfortable and easy experience. All cooking appliances and utensils you’d need and clean linen and towels. We all slept like logs as it was lovely and warm and peaceful as it has heating too. Just got back from a couple of nights at Dilham Hall Retreats. We stayed at the Couples Glamping site in Swallowtail.

The pod itself provided all we needed and was spotless. Communication with the owners was excellent. There were some lovely walks available from the site. Highlight had to be the hot tub. It was a magical experience to be sitting in there watching the barn owl and bats flying around and listening to the Tawny owls calling to each other. We were also visited by a beautiful Reeves pheasant. It was an amazing place to say which I would highly recommend.
