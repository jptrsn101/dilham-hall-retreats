About Dragonfly

Dragonfly sits deepest in the woodland of the three Broad Fen pods, which makes it the most private and the quietest. Soak in the wood-fired hot tub with nothing but trees and birdsong around you.

It has everything you need for a slow few days: a full kitchen, an en-suite shower, central heating for the cooler months and a king-size bed with bedding and towels supplied. Light the fire pit and stay up late under the stars.

What is inside

  • Wood-fired hot tub
  • Full kitchen
  • En-suite shower & WC
  • Smart TV & Wi-Fi
  • Fire pit with grill
  • Central heating

Adults only, no under 18s. No dogs at Broad Fen. One complimentary bag of wood, extra bags available.
