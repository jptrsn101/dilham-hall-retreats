# Photo checklist — Dilham Hall Retreats

This is the list of every photo the website is waiting for. Each one has a **fixed filename** in the `assets/images/` folder.

## How to use it (the easy bit)
1. Pick a photo from your camera/phone.
2. Save it with the **exact filename** listed below (lowercase, `.jpg`, no spaces).
3. Drop it into `assets/images/`, replacing the placeholder that is already there.

It will then appear in the right place on the site automatically. You do **not** need to do them all at once. Replace them whenever you like, in any order, and nothing breaks in the meantime (the current placeholder stays until you swap it).

**Recommended:** landscape, about 2000px wide, good natural light. Keep each file under ~600KB if you can (it keeps the site fast). ⭐ = please do these first, they matter most.
