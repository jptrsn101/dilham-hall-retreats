Canal Camping & Bell Tents

The simple stuff done well. Bring your own tent and pick a spacious pitch, or hire a ready‑pitched family bell tent with beds, a fire pit and everything you need. Hot showers, campfires and the canal right there.

  • Own tent or bell tent
  • Children welcome
  • Dogs, £5 a stay
  • No hot tubs
  • Self‑launch £5 a vessel
  • 3 night minimum

What to expect

Roomy 6‑metre family bell tents come fully kitted out with beds and bedding, a table and chairs, cooking kit and a fire pit. Prefer your own canvas? Pitch up on a spacious, open spot by the canal. Either way you get hot showers, drinking water, campfire braziers and a canoe launch on site.

  • Hot showers
  • Campfire braziers
  • Drinking water
  • Canoe launch
