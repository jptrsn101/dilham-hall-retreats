About Swallowtail

Swallowtail has the most open outlook of the three, with the widest view across the fen meadow from the hot tub. In summer you may even spot the rare swallowtail butterfly it is named after.

Like its neighbours it is finished to a high standard, with a full kitchen, en-suite shower, central heating and a king-size bed with bedding and towels. A fire pit and grill sit just outside for long, lazy evenings.

What is inside

  • Wood-fired hot tub
  • Full kitchen
  • En-suite shower & WC
  • Smart TV & Wi-Fi
  • Fire pit with grill
  • Central heating

Adults only, no under 18s. No dogs at Broad Fen. One complimentary bag of wood, extra bags available.
