Couples glamping in a 95‑acre nature reserve

Three secluded pods for two, set deep in woodland and fen meadow. Wood‑fired hot tub, proper kitchen and total privacy. Made for a romantic escape.

Our story

A working farm, looked after for generations.

The Paterson family have farmed this land since 1949. Luke, a fifth‑generation farmer, and his wife Louise built the retreats to share it with you while caring for the nature that makes it special. Everything here is designed around the land, not against it.
